# Privacy Policy for Tracker BabyFlow

Last updated: November 2025  

Tracker BabyFlow (“the App”) is developed by Kirill Khanzhinov.  
This document explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

---

## 1. Data We Collect
Tracker BabyFlow does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All data you create inside the App — including:
- child profiles
- feeding logs
- sleep logs
- diaper/toilet logs
- notes
- settings

is stored **locally on your device only**.

We do not use external databases, analytics, or tracking systems.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services
The App does **not** integrate any third-party services that collect personal information.

---

## 3. Data Sharing
We do **not** share your data with:
- third parties  
- partners  
- advertisers  
- analytics services  

We cannot access your data in any way.

---

## 4. Data Security
All information is stored securely on your device using standard iOS data protection mechanisms.

Deleting the App permanently deletes all stored data.

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy
The App is intended for parents.  
We do not collect data directly from children.
