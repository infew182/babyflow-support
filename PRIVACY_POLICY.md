# Privacy Policy for Tracker BabyFlow

This Privacy Policy describes how the BabyFlow app handles your data.

## Data Collection
Tracker BabyFlow does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data to external servers.  
All information—including feeding logs, sleep logs, diaper logs, child profiles and settings—is stored **locally on your device only**.

## Data Sharing
Tracker BabyFlow does not share any information with third parties.

## Data Retention
All data remains on your device until you manually remove the app or delete the data in the settings.
